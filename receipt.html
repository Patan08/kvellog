Kellogg's Granola Parfait

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Kellogg's Granola Crunch
  • 1 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup mixed fresh fruits (such as berries, kiwi, and mango)
  • 2 tablespoons honey or maple syrup

Instructions:

  • In a glass or bowl, layer half of the granola, followed by half of the Greek yogurt.
  • Add a layer of mixed fruits.
  • Repeat the layering with the remaining granola, yogurt, and fruits.
  • Drizzle honey or maple syrup on top.
  • Serve immediately or refrigerate for up to an hour for a chilled parfait.

Serving Size: 1 parfait

Preparation Time: 10 minutes

Classic Kellogg's Corn Flakes Breakfast

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Kellogg's Corn Flakes
  • 1/2 cup milk (or plant-based milk)
  • 1/2 banana, sliced
  • 1/4 cup fresh berries (strawberries, blueberries, or raspberries)
  • 1 tablespoon honey (optional)

Instructions:

  • Pour Kellogg's Corn Flakes into a bowl.
  • Add milk to the bowl.
  • Top with sliced banana and fresh berries.
  • Drizzle honey over the top if desired.
  • Serve immediately and enjoy!

Serving Size: 1 bowl

Preparation Time: 5 minutes

VKellogg's Savory Oatmeal

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Kellogg's Oat Flakes
  • 2 cups water or vegetable broth
  • 1/2 avocado, sliced
  • 1 poached egg
  • 1/4 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 tablespoon chopped chives
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  • In a pot, bring water or vegetable broth to a boil.
  • Add Kellogg's Oat Flakes and reduce the heat to simmer.
  • Cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally until the oats are tender and the liquid is absorbed.
  • Transfer the cooked oats to a bowl.
  • Top with sliced avocado, poached egg, cherry tomatoes, and chopped chives.
  • Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  • Serve immediately and enjoy a healthy, savory breakfast!

Serving Size: 1 bowl

Preparation Time: 15 minutes
